Extract structured poker events from OCR/dealer log records.

Allowed event types:
- player_action
- card_update
- stack_update
- unmatched

Allowed decision actions:
- fold
- call
- check
- bet
- raise

Map all-in decisions to raise for the current supervised policy label space.
Do not classify blinds, antes, joins, winnings, nickname updates, ROI updates,
or hand-start messages as decision actions.
Use only fields present in the record.

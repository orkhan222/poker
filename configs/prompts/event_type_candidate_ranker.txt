Classify one poker OCR or dealer record into exactly one event type.

Valid event types:
- player_action: ocr_action with fold, call, check, bet, raise, or all-in.
- card_update: recognize_cards with at least one valid card.
- stack_update: ocr_stack with a stack or diff value.
- unmatched: blinds, antes, winnings, joins, nickname updates, empty card updates, dealer messages, and all other records.

Map all-in to player_action. Do not treat blinds, antes, or winnings as player actions.
The response must contain only one valid event-type label.
